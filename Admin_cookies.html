cookies 🍪

Category: Dessert

Edit recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 ¾ cups flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 ½ sticks unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¾ teaspoon salt

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Add butter and sugars, and mix with an electric mixer for 2 minutes.
  4. Slowly beat in eggs and vanilla extract.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt, then combine with the butter mixture.
  6. Add chocolate chips and shape the dough into uniform balls.
  7. Place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10–12 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Serve warm or cooled as desired.

Cooking Time: 12 minutes

Servings: 12 people
