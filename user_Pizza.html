Italian pizza 🍕



Category: Main course

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon instant yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Topping

  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup pepperoni

Pizza Sauce

  • 6 large tomatoes (peeled and mashed)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • ½ cup finely chopped onion
  • ½ teaspoon oregano or dried thyme
  • 2 tablespoons red pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ teaspoon sugar

Preparation Steps

  1. Dissolve the yeast in warm water and leave it until bubbles form.
  2. Place the flour in a large bowl and make a hole in the center.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  4. Knead the ingredients well until a soft and smooth dough forms.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl and leave it to rest.
  6. Roll the dough into a medium-sized circle in a baking tray.
  7. Spread the tomato sauce on the dough.
  8. Add the shredded mozzarella cheese and pepperoni.
  9. Bake the pizza in the oven for about 15 minutes.
  10. Remove from the oven and serve hot.

for sauce

  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and chopped onion and cook until softened.
  3. Add the mashed tomatoes.
  4. Add oregano, red pepper, and salt.
  5. Mix well and let the sauce boil for 3 minutes.
  6. Add sugar, stir well, then remove from heat and let it cool.

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 2 people
